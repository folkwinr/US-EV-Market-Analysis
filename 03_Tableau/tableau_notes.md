# Tableau Dashboard Notes

### Dashboard 1 — U.S. EV Overview
Contains:
- National KPIs (Total Vehicles, Total EVs, EV Adoption Rate)
- State-level EV adoption map
- EV performance vs U.S. average
- Top 10 EV states
- California vs large states comparison

### Dashboard 2 — Regional Electrification
Contains:
- Regional EV/PHEV/HEV heatmap
- Region EV % vs national EV %
- EV share vs total fleet (bubble chart)
- Regional EV share (bar chart)

### Dashboard 3 — Alternative Fuel Overview
Contains:
- Fuel type distribution across states
- Total alternative fuel usage by category
- Top 5 states for each niche fuel
- Vehicle fleet composition pie charts

Design Notes:
- All colors follow consistent palette
- Icons represent EV, PHEV, HEV, Alt Fuel
- Dashboards optimized for 1920 × 1080 resolution
